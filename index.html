WHAT IS
SCALE-UP HEALTH?

India's first community for healthcare & life sciences leaders.

Scale-Up Health has become a cornerstone gathering for healthcare and life sciences leaders, drawing over 180 senior executives, founders, and policy experts in 2024. This year, we're looking forward to a bigger more insightful event.

The theme this year is India's Healthcare Decade: Innovation, Expansion & Global Influence, which will explore the country's emerging leadership in global healthcare innovation, delivery, and biopharma advancement.

At Scale-Up Health, you can build new relationships, gain deep insights and be part of a thriving ecosystem who come together with a unified vision. Will you be there?
